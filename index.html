Connecting you to the world: Connectify

A contact book manager web application is a tool used to organize and manage contact information in one central location. It allows users to store and edit contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and other relevant information. The application is accessible online, making it easy for users to access their contacts from any device with an internet connection.
